We specialize in the documentation of artworks and installations, from wall-long murals to kinetic sculptures, from ink drawing to performances nights. And we do it on site, whether at your studio or at the gallery. Our goal is to provide you with high resolution pictures or videos of your pieces, ready to be used and disseminated online. Think of us as a one step, personalized archival system, enabling you to show your work without compromising quality or taking time off to do it.

After planning a session, we will show up with the required equipment (lightings, high-end cameras, recorders) and begin the documentation. After processing the results, we will provide you with a link and a DVD to download what was captured. You obviously gain ownership of the pictures and are then free to use them as you wish. We also offer post-processing services if needed, even if it's all you need for an existing portfolio (color calibration, cropping, resizing, converting).
